Dagmar Glemme Art Center

"What would an artists home be without its own gallery?"

This is what Dagmar thought when she moved to her house in the paradise-like village of Ugglarp, located on the Swedish west coast.

Here in Ugglarp is not only Dagmars home but also an art center open for visitors where one can enjoy an array of vibrant colors, mind-stretching motifs and a stroll in the well decorated garden. There are many seating groups situated in the garden, so please feel free to bring your own snacks to enjoy.

Feel like visiting?

To visit Dagmar Glemme Art Center please reach out via phone (+46 70 778 31 19) or email (dagmar.glemme@gmail.com) beforehand to make an appointment and Dagmar will greet you with warmth and personally guide you threw her oasis of colors.

Are you a group of up to 50 people?

No problem! Dagmar, with assistance of her grandchildren Alexander and Benjamin, can guide you and organize your visit accordingly.

Parking Space

Right across the road from DGAC is home of the great car and aeroplane museum Svedinos. Svedinos has a big parking lot with free parking for visitors of DGAC, so please feel free to park anywhere inside the compounds of Svedinos.
